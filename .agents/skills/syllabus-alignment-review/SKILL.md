# Syllabus Alignment Review Skill

## Inputs to read first

- `AGENTS.md`
- `docs/codex/MOE_SYLLABUS_ALIGNMENT.md`
- `docs/codex/HISTORY_CONTENT_CONTEXT.md`
- `docs/codex/CONTENT_AUTHORING_GUIDE.md`

## Review workflow

1. Identify the chapter or scene being reviewed.
2. Confirm the syllabus unit and topic.
3. Identify the historical concepts being taught.
4. Check whether the interaction requires inquiry, evidence, perspective-taking, or causation reasoning.
5. Check whether the assessment evidence is observable in player action.
6. Flag unsupported historical claims for verification.
7. Flag excessive focus on WWII military campaigns if it distracts from syllabus goals.
8. Suggest a concise revision that improves syllabus alignment.

## Output format

```md
## Syllabus alignment review

Verdict: aligned / partially aligned / not aligned

Matched syllabus topic:
Historical concepts used:
Learning outcomes supported:
Assessment evidence:
Issues:
Recommended changes:
```

## Key guardrails

- Do not accept trivia-only tasks as strong learning design.
- Do not accept one-sided causal explanations.
- Do not accept scenes that glorify war, authoritarianism, or violence.
- Do not overbuild military-campaign detail.
- Require source/evidence reasoning where possible.
