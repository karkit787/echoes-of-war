# History Chapter Writer Skill

Follow this workflow whenever drafting or revising story content for Echoes of War.

## Inputs to read first

- `AGENTS.md`
- `docs/codex/PROJECT_BRIEF.md`
- `docs/codex/HISTORY_CONTENT_CONTEXT.md`
- `docs/codex/CONTENT_AUTHORING_GUIDE.md`

## Workflow

1. Identify the chapter and learning objective.
2. List historical anchors that must remain accurate.
3. Draft scenes using the scene template.
4. Include at least one investigation moment and one decision point.
5. Add companion reactions for Logic, Empathy, Scepticism, Memory, and Conscience where appropriate.
6. Add fallback hints for wrong or hesitant player responses.
7. End each scene with a concise learning reflection.
8. Mark any historical detail that needs verification rather than inventing certainty.

## Output expectations

For narrative documents, output clear Markdown.
For implementation-ready content, output structured TypeScript/JSON-like data compatible with the project types.

## Tone rules

- Historically grounded.
- Suitable for secondary school learners.
- Serious but engaging.
- No glorification of war or extremist ideology.

## Additional required references

Also read:

- `docs/codex/MOE_SYLLABUS_ALIGNMENT.md`
- `docs/codex/DESIGN_REFERENCE_DISCO_ELYSIUM.md`

When drafting chapters, include a curriculum alignment block and ensure decisions test historical reasoning rather than trivia only.
